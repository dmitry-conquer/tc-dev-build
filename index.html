About TruCare Home Care

When we established TruCare, we committed to one guiding principle: delivering the highest quality of professional, compassionate care to those who need it right in the comfort of their own homes. This principle has steered us in every decision, every interaction, and every comprehensive care plan from that day till now.

We began our journey years ago with a straightforward mission—to create a comfortable and familiar environment for those needing assistance in caring for their daily activities. Over time, we grew, but our mission remained the same. We expanded our quality home care services in Philadelphia, PA, and its surrounding areas, assisting countless individuals and their families and enabling them to lead comfortable, fulfilling lives in their homes.

Our qualified caregivers—the heart and soul of our work—were selected meticulously. They are handpicked with ultra careful attention to both their professional qualifications and personal traits. They exhibit dedication, empathy, and a commitment to the well-being of their clients.

What Makes Us Stand Out?

Serving Various Counties

We are not only the “Philadephia Home Care Agency”. We proudly serve people in need in Montgomery County, Bucks County, Reading, PA County, Chester County, Delaware County, Pottstown, and other surrounding areas. This wide coverage allows us to touch more lives and deliver quality senior care services and to other individuals in need.

Commitment to Quality Care for People

At TruCare Home Care, we strive to provide long-term care and peace of mind for families. This involves creating personalized care plans that cater to the unique needs of each individual we serve. Our home care assistance in Philadelphia and surrounding areas focuses on promoting independence, enhancing quality of life, and ensuring safety for our clients.

Our caregivers undergo rigorous reference checks to ensure they are trained, compassionate, and reliable. We also offer private pay options to cater to various financial needs.

Positive Reviews from Clients

Our commitment to quality of care has earned us positive reviews from clients across Montgomery County, Bucks County, Reading, PA County, Chester County, Delaware County, and Pottstown. These testimonials affirm our efforts to provide top-notch home care services, making us a preferred choice for many families.

Recognition in the Community

Our dedication to improving the lives of seniors and their families hasn't gone unnoticed. We're proud to be recognized by various community organizations for our commitment to providing a wide range of services as one of the leading home care agencies in PA.
